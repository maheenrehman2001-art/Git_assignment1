Space Facts

  • The Sun is 109 times wider than Earth!
  • Jupiter has 79 known moons orbiting it.
  • A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus.
  • Because there is no atmosphere in space, sound has no medium to travel through to be heard.
  • Venus is hotter than Mercury due to its thick atmosphere trapping heat in a greenhouse effect.
  • The footprints on the Moon will stay there for 100 million years because there is no wind or water to erode them.
  • A day on Mercury lasts 59 Earth days.
